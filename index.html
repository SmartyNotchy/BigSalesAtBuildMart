Big Sales At Build Mart

It's Black Friday, and business is booming at Build Mart, Inc! Hand-drawn paintings are proving to be especially popular, and the supply of them has quickly run out. As the only artist in Build Mart, you'll have to keep up with the painting orders as they come in. Build Mart closes in five minutes, though, so people are impatient to get their paintings and will pay less for them the longer you take. Try to fulfill as many orders as possible before the five minutes run out!

TL;DR: Make as many paintings as possible in five minutes. Values of paintings decrease the longer you spend on them.

Controls

WASD or Arrow Keys - Move Paintbrush (Outlined By Gray Square)

Colored Buttons or 123456789 - Change Color

Space - Paint Selected Color

You can undo mistakes by painting over them with a different colored paint (usually white.)

Once your canvas (the right painting) matches the model painting on the left, the painting will automatically be sold and replaced with a new one.
